Balatapa

[Sanskrit]

1. Heat of the morning sun

Inspired by our Balatapa Print.

With the arrival of the Sun’s rays in the morning, photosynthesis begins. Through this process, plants take in carbon dioxide from the air and make oxygen for us all to breathe. This design is a celebration of the beauty and warmth of the morning sun and all the energy it brings to us.

Details

  • Designed and embroidered with love in London, UK
  • 85% Organic super soft ring-spun Combed Cotton, 15% Recycled Polyester
  • 350 GSM Heavyweight Premium Quality
  • Made in Bangladesh in a Fairwear Factory
  • Unisex Garment

Sizing

The garment is true to size and similar fit to other Idioma hoods and sweats. Buy your usual size. US customers consider going up a size.
